Product Details

Introducing the luxurious and captivating Frank Olivier Oud Touch perfume, a fragrance that exudes confidence and sophistication with every spritz.Crafted with the finest ingredients, this scent opens with a burst of citrusy top notes, featuring zesty bergamot and invigorating lemon, which are balanced by warm and spicy middle notes of saffron and black pepper. But what truly sets this perfume apart is its rich and exotic base note of oud, also known as agarwood, a rare and highly prized ingredient that imparts a deep and woody aroma with hints of leather and tobacco. With its long-lasting and intense fragrance, Frank Olivier Oud Touch is perfect for evening wear or any special occasion. Whether you're dressing up for a night out or simply want to feel confident and alluring, this perfume will help you make a statement and leave a lasting impression. Indulge in the luxury of Frank Olivier Oud Touch and experience the timeless appeal of oud in a modern and refined way.
