Product Details

Blue Oud is a luxurious and unique fragrance from the esteemed perfume brand Lataffa. This scent opens with a fresh and citrusy burst of top notes featuring bergamot and lemon, which are beautifully balanced by the floral and spicy middle notes of jasmine and black pepper. The base notes of agarwood (oud), sandalwood, and musk create a rich and woody finish that will transport you to exotic lands. With its long-lasting and complex fragrance, Blue Oud is perfect for any occasion - from a casual day out to a formal evening event. Whether you're dressing up or down, this perfume will help you feel confident and stylish. The sleek and sophisticated bottle design also adds a touch of elegance to any fragrance collection. Indulge in the luxurious blend of fresh citrus, exotic florals, and rich oud with this captivating and unforgettable scent. Experience the allure of Blue Oud by Lataffa and embrace the power of fragrance to transport you to distant lands and captivate the senses.
