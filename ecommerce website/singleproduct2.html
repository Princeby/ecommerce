Product Details

Introducing Frank Olivier Oud Vanille, an opulent and sensual fragrance that combines the richness of oud with the sweetness of vanilla for a truly unforgettable scent experience. This perfume opens with a burst of citrusy bergamot and refreshing lemon, which then give way to a heart of warm and spicy notes like cinnamon and nutmeg. But it's the base notes of oud and vanilla that truly set this fragrance apart, creating a luxurious and velvety finish that lingers on the skin for hours. Perfect for any occasion, Frank Olivier Oud Vanille is a versatile scent that can be worn both day and night. Whether you're looking to make a statement or simply want to add a touch of sophistication to your everyday routine, this perfume is sure to impress. With its sleek and stylish bottle design, Frank Olivier Oud Vanille also makes a beautiful addition to any fragrance collection. Indulge in the luxurious blend of oud and vanilla with this captivating and unforgettable scent.
